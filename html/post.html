The Fun in Legos

Here are a few reasons why everyone should play with Legos!

  1. Legos Ignite Creativity
    • Legos allow children and adults of all ages to bring their wildest ideas to life.
    • Within the themed sets offered like space shuttles, or ships, even castles, Legos encourage and support the development of narrative skills for kids as they role-play.
  2. Legos Provide Educational Values
    • STEM learning is incorporated within Legos offering an engaging and hands-on method of learning.
    • Legos can help develop fine motor skills as Lego pieces require motor skills and hand-eye coordination to connect them.
  3. Legos Connect to Films
    • Lego sets inspired by these Lego movies and TV shows inspired interactive play where watchers can replicate and even continue the storylines in real life.
    • Seeing Legos as the main animation within movies and TV shows support the Lego brand of creativity and imagination seeing what would be blocks come to screen and offer rich storylines and character developments.

Legos are versatile in their many sets accommodating for young kids and adults!
